## Why Notion Widgets Beat Separate Productivity Apps

The core problem with productivity setups isn't a shortage of tools — it's context-switching. You open Notion to plan your day, then jump to a separate Pomodoro app, then check your habit tracker in another tab, then open a weather app to plan your week. Each switch costs focus.

Notion widgets solve this by embedding interactive tools directly into your pages as iframes. Your timer runs next to your task list. Your habit tracker sits below your daily journal.
