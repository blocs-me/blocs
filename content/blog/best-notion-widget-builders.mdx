## What Is a Notion Widget Builder?

A Notion widget builder is a web tool that generates embeddable mini-apps you can drop into any Notion page. Because Notion supports iframe embeds natively, any URL that renders a self-contained app can be added to your workspace in seconds.

Widget builders typically offer things like timers, habit trackers, clocks, calendars, weather displays, and quote feeds — functionality Notion doesn't provide out of the box. The best ones are designed specifically with Notion's embed dimensions in mind, so they look clean and proportional rather than cramped or broken.

## How to Embed a Widget in Notion

The process is the same regardless of which builder you use:

1. Go to the widget builder and configure your widget (size, theme, goals)
2. Copy the embed URL provided
3. Open your Notion page and type `/embed`
4. Paste the URL and press Enter
5. Resize the embed block to fit your layout

With Blocs, you don't even need to configure anything for the free widgets — the default URL works immediately. Visit [blocs.me/pomodoro-timer](https://blocs.me/pomodoro-timer), copy the embed link, and paste it into Notion.
