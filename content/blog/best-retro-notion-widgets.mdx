## What Are Retro Notion Widgets?

Retro Notion widgets are embeddable mini-apps that bring vintage visual design into your Notion pages. Think flip-digit clocks, analog-inspired timers, warm earth-tone trackers, and typography that echoes an earlier era of computing or analog desk accessories.

Unlike standard Notion databases or templates, these widgets are live and interactive. They're embedded using Notion's native **/embed** block by pasting a widget URL. The result: a workspace that looks handcrafted and feels distinctly yours, without any plugins or third-party integrations.

The appeal is more than cosmetic. A retro aesthetic slows things down visually, reduces screen fatigue, and creates the kind of focused atmosphere that lo-fi playlists have long delivered aurally. It's productivity design that respects your attention.
