## What Is a Budget Widget for Notion?

A budget widget for Notion would be an embeddable tool — typically an iframe — that lets you set a spending limit, log expenses, and visualize your financial progress directly inside a Notion page. Instead of jumping between a banking app, a spreadsheet, and your Notion workspace, everything lives in one place.

## How to Set Up a Simple Budget Dashboard in Notion

1. **Create a new Notion page** — call it "Monthly Budget" or "Finance Dashboard."
2. **Add a database** — use a table with columns: Category, Budgeted Amount, Actual Spend, Difference.
3. **Add a formula column** — use `prop("Budgeted Amount") - prop("Actual Spend")` to calculate remaining.
4. **Embed a Progress Bar** — type `/embed`, paste `https://blocs.me/progress-bar`, set your monthly limit as the goal.
5. **Add a Countdown Timer** — embed `https://blocs.me/countdown` and set it to your next payday or bill date.
6. **Review weekly** — use Blocs' built-in analytics (Pro) to track patterns over time.
