## How to Embed a Blocs Widget in Notion

Embedding any Blocs widget takes under a minute:

1. Go to [blocs.me](https://blocs.me) and open the widget you want (e.g., `blocs.me/pomodoro`).
2. Copy the full URL from your browser's address bar.
3. In Notion, type `/embed` and select the Embed block.
4. Paste the Blocs widget URL and click Embed.
5. Resize the block to fit your layout.

That's it. The widget is live, interactive, and persists your data as long as you use the same browser (or across devices with a Pro account and cloud sync enabled).
