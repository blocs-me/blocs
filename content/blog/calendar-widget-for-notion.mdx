## How to Add a Calendar Widget to Notion

Embedding any Blocs widget in Notion takes the same three steps every time:

3. **Resize as needed.** Drag the embed block handles to fit the calendar neatly on your page.

The calendar renders immediately inside Notion. You don't need to sign in to try it, though a Blocs Pro account unlocks customization and additional features.
