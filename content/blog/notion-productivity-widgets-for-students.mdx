## How to Embed a Blocs Widget in Notion

Embedding any Blocs widget takes about 60 seconds. Here's how:

1. Go to the widget page on [blocs.me](https://blocs.me) (for example, `blocs.me/pomodoro-timer`).
2. Copy the embed URL from the page (e.g., `https://blocs.me/pomodoro`).
3. Open your Notion page and type `/embed` to insert an embed block.
4. Paste the URL and click **Embed link**.
5. Resize the block to fit your layout.

No account required for free widgets. For Pro widgets, sign in at [blocs.me/sign-in](https://blocs.me/sign-in) first so your data syncs across devices.
